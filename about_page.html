About Me

I am a second year student at Singapore Polytechnic studying Mechatronics and Robotics. I enjoy designing and making things. You can often find me designing parts in Fusion 360 before printing them on my 3D printer. I chose the EP1000 module because it would allow me to pick up new skills like laser cutting and programming a website. This opens up more ways for me to build my projects and document them online.
